Sweep Key Takeaways

This sweep helps locate the crossover point for the QJL residual at $d=64$. While QJL yields unbiased dot products, the added 1-bit sketch and secondary norms introduce additional variance and storage overhead at higher bit budgets.

At lower bits (e.g., 2-bit), the unbiased nature of QJL helps prevent severe perplexity spikes, whereas at higher bits (5-bit), MSE-only quantization achieves superior rate-distortion performance since the coordinate-wise quantization error is already small.
